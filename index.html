PseudoNetCDF User’s Guide

About

The key value of PseudoNetCDF is netcdf-like access to complex data formats in the air quality field.

PseudoNetCDF was created to provide netcdf-like (see netCDF4) access air quality data written in other formats. The interface was designed based on Scientific.io.netcdf and netcdf4-python. Then it grew to provide meta-data aware spatial and temporal processing, some inspired by xarray and pandas.
